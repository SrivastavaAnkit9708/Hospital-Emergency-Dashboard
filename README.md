## **Features & Highlights**  
- **Top Metrics Overview**: Total patients, average wait time, satisfaction score, referrals  
- **Admission Status Breakdown**: Admitted vs. Not Admitted  
- **Wait Time Target Analysis**: % of patients seen within 30 minutes  
- **Demographic Insights**: Age group, gender, race distributions  
- **Referral Patterns**: Department-wise patient referrals  
- **Temporal Trends**: Patient volume by day and hour (heatmap)  
- **Visual Design**: Clean layout with bar charts, pie charts, and heatmaps for intuitive analysis
